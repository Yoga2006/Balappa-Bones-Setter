About Us

Balappa Bones Setter is a family-run traditional healing center located in Proddatur, Andhra Pradesh. For generations, our ancestors have practiced the sacred art of natural bone setting and healing, serving people with dedication, compassion, and ancient knowledge.

We specialize in healing bone fractures, dislocations, and joint pain using non-invasive techniques rooted in Ayurvedic practices. Our remedies involve the use of herbal oils, hand massages, bamboo splints, and cloth bandaging. We are proud to have helped thousands regain their health without surgery or synthetic medicine.

At Balappa Bones Setter, we believe in accessible and affordable care, especially for people from rural and underprivileged backgrounds. Our mission is to blend ancient wisdom with modern compassion to heal every patient who walks through our doors.
